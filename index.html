About us

We get things done!

Our goals

  • Organize open data from Kaunas city municipality to make it universally accessible and useful.
  • Use technology to create or enhance the communication between citizens and local government.
  • Encourage participants to recognize and strengthen our actions and their effects on our community.
  • Promote openness, participation and active citizenship.
  • Show what’s possible with technology today - Code for Kaunas!

How Can You Contribute?

  1. Introduce your idea for Kaunas city and let’s get started!
  2. Express your problem with the government and help us solve it.
  3. Join projects that are already in progress.
  4. Share your knowledge and expertise.
  5. Help organize projects and events.
  6. Sponsor our community organization.
  7. Help us spread our ideas through social media.
